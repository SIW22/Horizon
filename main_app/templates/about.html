About Horizon

Now, more than ever, we all need something to look forward to. So the team at JSM Designs got together and decided to give people hope and positivity, however they needed it. And all of us, no matter who we are or what we're doing, need something on the Horizon.

Horizon is a simple, easy-to-use event tracking application, allowing users to keep track of whatever positive events in their lives they have coming up. Horizon walks the line between privacy and sharing, allowing users to add friends and connections to share events with, but still retain privacy-first tracking if they'd like.
